Elbow ROM Tracker

Measure elbow flexion, extension deficit, and arc of motion from lateral photos

Point-click elbow range of motion measurement

Take a new photo using your device camera or upload an existing image. Click three landmarks on a lateral elbow photo to calculate flexion and extension.

Photo and measurement instructions

How to take the photo

  1. Take the photo from the side of the elbow.
  2. Keep the shoulder/upper arm, elbow, forearm, and wrist visible if possible.
  3. Keep the camera perpendicular to the arm.
  4. Avoid oblique or angled shots.
  5. Use a partner or phone timer for better positioning.

Point-click landmarks

  1. Click a proximal humerus / upper-arm point.
  2. Click the elbow center.
  3. Click a distal forearm / wrist point.
  4. The app calculates the angle at the elbow.
Dataset-first design: Every finished measurement can export JSON landmark labels for future automation with pose estimation or a custom keypoint model.
